# Physiconfig, Not Disorder

## Support for Stable Forms in Polytraitspace

**Version:** v0.004  
**Date:** 2026-07-04  
**Status:** hypothesis architecture / support-language instrument. Not medical advice.

Local rule:

```text
support_need != defect_verdict
```

Keeper:

```text
Support the route that carries cost.
Do not destroy the form just because the route costs.
```

## Abstract

A person can need support without their base configuration being on trial.

This paper names the distinction **physiconfig**: a stable body-brain-form in multidimensional trait space. A physiconfig may be costly under load, disabled by a setting, risky in some states, helped by medicine, eligible for services, or protected by rights action. None of those facts, by itself, proves that the base form is a broken average.

Physiconfig is not a diagnosis. It is not a replacement taxonomy. It is not a verdict that a form is harmless, easy, untreated, or above help. It is an audit handle.

The paper separates four objects that are often collapsed:

```text
FORM;
COST;
MISMATCH;
SERVICE_GATE.
```

A clinical label may attach at the service gate. A support need may be real. Disability may be real. Crisis may be real. Medication, therapy, accommodation, rights action, and emergency care may help. Reduction may be chosen.

The error is letting support need, observer discomfort, service label, or crisis contact testify that the whole form is defective.

The opposite error is also blocked. Valid-form language may not be used to deny care, hide acute illness, reject medicine, excuse danger, or trap the person inside untreated cost.

Short public sentence:

```text
Some people are not broken versions of average.
They are stable forms that need the right support,
and sometimes treatment for the costs.
```

## 1. One-page answer

Physiconfig is a custody rule for support language.

It blocks this collapse:

```text
support_need
-> disorder_essence
-> correction_toward_average
```

It also blocks the opposite collapse:

```text
valid_form
-> no_care_needed
-> no_medicine_allowed
-> crisis_ignored
```

The better route is:

```text
difference
-> map_form
-> check_person_cost
-> check_observer_cost
-> check_mismatch
-> check_danger_and_acute_change
-> ask_person_desire
-> add_fit_and_support
-> treat_cost_when_wanted_or_needed
-> use_service_label_without_letting_it_own_the_person
```

The core split:

```text
form != cost
cost != mismatch
mismatch != service_gate
service_gate != essence
```

The safety rule:

```text
valid_form != low_cost
valid_form != always_safe
valid_form != no_treatment
valid_form != no_reduction
valid_form != no_crisis_plan
```

The support aim is not:

```text
make person average.
```

The support aim is:

```text
help the person live, choose, recover, communicate, work, love, rest,
and stay safe without making help testify against the form.
```

## 3. Decision rights of the handle

Physiconfig has limited decision rights.

It may:

```text
orient;
split form from cost;
split person-cost from observer-cost;
block default defect verdicts;
ask for fit before erasure;
ask for desire before settled intervention;
require acute-state and danger checks.
```

It may not:

```text
diagnose;
deny impairment;
deny disability;
deny medicine;
deny crisis;
declare every cost social;
declare every reduction erasure;
override the person's desired change;
override urgent safety care.
```

Compact:

```text
physiconfig may block overclaim.
physiconfig may not block care.
```

## 4. The four-object split

Most bad support grammar collapses four objects.

```text
FORM + COST + MISMATCH + SERVICE_GATE
=> one_disorder_object
```

Physiconfig keeps them apart.

```text
FORM:
  what stable configuration is present?

COST:
  what does the configuration cost the person under actual conditions?

MISMATCH:
  what setting increases that cost?

SERVICE_GATE:
  what label, pathway, or documentation unlocks support?
```

The label may attach at the service gate.

The form does not thereby become an error.

This split is not anti-clinical. It is a way to use clinical tools without letting the tools eat the person-form.

## 5. Acute-state boundary

Physiconfig must not become a shield against care.

The frame narrows when:

```text
pattern_is_new;
pattern_is_progressive;
pattern_is_toxic;
pattern_is_infectious;
pattern_is_degenerative;
pattern_is_traumatic_injury;
pattern_is_medically_unstable;
sleep_food_water_are_collapsing;
danger_or_coercion_is_rising;
return_path_is_lost;
person_wants_reduction_or_treatment;
```

In those cases, the order changes.

```text
first:
  stabilize body, danger, consent, sleep, food, water, and return path

then:
  ask what stable form remains
```

Valid-form language may never be used to trap a person inside untreated crisis.

Medicine can support form.

Crisis care is not form betrayal.

Reduction can be chosen.

Treatment can be a way the form survives.

## 6. Severity ladder

Severity decides support posture. It does not decide essence.

```text
GREEN={
  stable_form;
  manageable_person_cost;
  return_path_intact;
  consent_intact;
  no_imminent_danger;
  fit_and_support_planning;
}
```

```text
AMBER={
  rising_person_cost;
  burnout_risk;
  mismatch_increasing;
  sleep_food_pain_or_stress_load_worsening;
  body_support_and_load_reduction_needed;
}
```

```text
RED={
  danger;
  coercion;
  exploitation;
  severe_self_neglect;
  collapse_of_sleep_food_water;
  escalating_injury_risk;
  return_path_fragile_or_lost;
  crisis_plan_needed;
}
```

```text
BLACK={
  immediate_medical_or_safety_emergency;
  stabilize_first;
  form_audit_later;
}
```

This ladder blocks two wrong moves:

```text
wrong_move_1:
  stable difference treated as emergency.

wrong_move_2:
  emergency treated as identity.
```

## 7. Observer-cost versus person-cost

A major capture route is observer-cost.

```text
observer_cost =
  the behaviour costs the institution, family, school, workplace,
  clinician, police route, service, or public norm.

person_cost =
  the route costs the person in body, access, pain, sleep, fear,
  dignity, consent, energy, safety, choice, or life.
```

They can overlap.

They must not be merged.

Bad move:

```text
observer is uncomfortable
-> person is clinically wrong
```

Better move:

```text
who pays the cost?
what kind of cost?
under what setting?
what support changes person-cost without erasing the form?
what observer-cost is just norm pressure?
what observer-cost also signals real risk?
```

Observer-cost may still matter. A school, ward, workplace, service, or family may need a plan. But observer-cost does not automatically become evidence of defect inside the person.

## 8. Valid form does not mean easy, safe, or untreated form

"Valid" is not a comfort word.

It does not mean:

```text
low_cost;
always_safe;
never_disabled;
never_medical;
never_risky;
never_changed;
never_supported;
never_treated;
never_reduced;
```

It means:

```text
do_not_condemn_base_form_from_support_need_alone.
```

A valid form can be in crisis.

A valid form can need medication.

A valid form can need major support.

A valid form can be incompatible with a setting.

A valid form can contain a route the person wants reduced.

The support answer is not:

```text
do nothing.
```

The support answer is:

```text
help without making help testify against the form.
```

## 9. Person-desire gate

Physiconfig fails if it protects the idea of a form while swallowing the person's say.

The desire gate comes before any settled support plan.

```text
DESIRE_GATE={
  what does the person want supported?
  what do they want reduced?
  what do they want protected?
  what do they want left alone?
  what do they want explained to others?
  what do they want kept private?
  what do they not know yet?
  what has been requested by others, not by the person?
  what costs are they willing to trade?
  what intervention would feel like erasure?
  what can they not safely answer yet?
}
```

The answer may be mixed.

A person may want sensory accommodations and medication for sleep.

A person may want tic acceptance in public and tic-reduction help for pain.

A person may want ADHD structure without shame and a medicine trial for task collapse.

A person may want autistic communication respected and panic treated.

A person may want an OCD-like checking route honoured as fear, while also reducing time capture.

Person desire does not erase danger.

Safety does not erase person desire.

Both must be carried.

## 10. Support ladder

Physiconfig support uses a ladder before erasure.

```text
1. Name the form without contempt.
2. Identify cost to the person, not only inconvenience to observers.
3. Identify observer-cost separately.
4. Identify environment mismatch.
5. Identify danger, coercion, consent, and acute-state risk.
6. Ask the person-desire gate.
7. Add fit: sensory, social, communicative, temporal, bodily.
8. Add translation: mutual when possible, assistive when needed.
9. Add load management: pacing, recovery, fewer stacked demands.
10. Add body support: sleep, food, pain, movement, medication, therapy, workup when indicated.
11. Add rights route: accommodation, advocacy, documentation, protection.
12. Add crisis plan if danger or return path fails.
13. Only then ask whether reducing trait expression is necessary, chosen, proportionate, and reversible.
```

Medicine is allowed.

Correction is allowed when locally needed.

Automatic norming is blocked.

## 11. When reduction is valid

Reducing trait expression can be valid when:

```text
person_wants_it;
cost_to_person_is_high;
danger_is_live;
support_and_fit_are_not_enough;
intervention_is_proportionate;
intervention_is_reversible_when_possible;
meaning_and_consent_are_preserved_when_possible;
acute_state_has_been_checked;
```

This prevents two errors.

```text
ERROR_1:
  erase form by reflex.

ERROR_2:
  forbid change because the form is valid.
```

Physiconfig is not anti-treatment.

It is anti-erasure-by-default.

## 12. Complex worked example

### Severe sensory gain at work

This is not a diagnosis. It is a route sketch.

```text
FORM:
  high sensory gain;
  pattern-hold and detail detection;
  fast pain response to sound, light, smell, crowding, and unpredictable interruption.

FORM_VALUE:
  notices errors early;
  detects pattern breaks;
  maintains high local accuracy;
  protects team from overlooked detail.

PERSON_COST:
  headaches;
  nausea;
  shutdown after meetings;
  sleep disruption after high-noise days;
  dread before open-office days;
  reduced eating and recovery;
  risk of burnout;
  fear of losing job.

OBSERVER_COST:
  colleagues find headphones unfriendly;
  manager dislikes camera-off meetings;
  team sees exit breaks as disengagement;
  workplace treats visible distress as morale problem;
  last-minute room-change refusal is read as attitude.

MISMATCH:
  open office;
  fluorescent lighting;
  hot-desking;
  sudden meetings;
  chat pings;
  no quiet recovery zone;
  spoken-only instructions;
  social lunch treated as loyalty signal.

SERVICE_GATE:
  occupational-health note;
  autism / sensory-processing / migraine / anxiety / stress label may unlock adjustments;
  the label is a door, not an essence verdict.

DESIRE_GATE:
  person wants to keep detailed work role;
  wants fewer overload days;
  wants headphones and quiet desk protected;
  wants sleep treated;
  does not want personality rewritten;
  is open to medication for migraine, anxiety, or sleep if it helps body cost;
  wants colleagues told only what is necessary.

SUPPORT:
  fixed desk;
  lighting change;
  headphones accepted;
  meeting agenda in advance;
  camera optional;
  written task channel;
  chat batching;
  quiet recovery room;
  fewer stacked deadlines;
  remote work days where possible;
  sleep and pain workup;
  migraine/anxiety/sleep medication trial if wanted and indicated;
  manager script and rights route.

CHOSEN_REDUCTION:
  reduce shutdown frequency;
  reduce pain;
  reduce panic before office days;
  reduce meeting overload;
  not reduce pattern-hold or detail detection by default.

BAD_MOVE:
  treat visible distress as proof of broken social attitude;
  treat all support as special favour;
  force exposure to prove normality;
  prescribe average behaviour as success;
  refuse medication because the form is valid.

BETTER_MOVE:
  protect form value;
  treat body cost;
  reduce mismatch;
  use label as access tool;
  let person decide which differences are supported, reduced, protected, or private.
```

Result:

```text
same route can have:
  form value;
  person-cost;
  observer-cost;
  mismatch;
  medicine option;
  rights option;
  chosen reduction.
```

That is why physiconfig needs object custody.

## 14. Relation to autism and eigenmode language

The companion autism paper proposes ASD as a central trait-space mode with ADD/ADHD, Tourette, OCD, and PDD-NOS-like boundary residue as neighbouring routes.

This physiconfig paper is broader and safer.

It does not depend on that four-node architecture being right.

If the autism-node model survives, physiconfig gives it support language.

If the autism-node model fails, physiconfig still holds the basic audit:

```text
form != cost != mismatch != service_gate
```

Safer public term:

```text
stable form
```

Internal modelling term:

```text
central polytrait basin
```

Eigenmode and basin language are modelling handles. They are not finished mathematical decomposition.

Claim:

```text
some clinically labelled patterns may be stable basins in polytraitspace.
```

Important split:

```text
distress_under_load != base_form_invalid
```

This matters most when the form is:

```text
old;
repeated;
identity_near;
body_deep;
not_voluntarily_adopted_as_strategy;
costly_to_suppress;
```

## 16. Export-safe language

### Clinician version

```text
This person may have a stable configuration that carries cost under current conditions.
Please separate form, person-cost, observer-cost, setting mismatch, acute-state risk,
and service need before treating the form as defect.

Support may include accommodation, therapy, medication, rights action, and crisis planning.
The label should unlock care, not define the whole person.
```

### Family version

```text
Support need does not prove someone is broken.
Ask what costs them, what costs you, what setting worsens it,
what help they want, and what needs urgent safety or medical attention.
Help can protect the person without trying to make them average.
```

### Service version

```text
Document the label needed for access, but do not let the access label become essence.
Record person-cost separately from observer-cost.
Record desired support and desired reduction separately.
Record acute risk separately from stable form.
```

### School and workplace version

```text
Do not convert observer inconvenience into person defect.
Audit environment, communication, pacing, recovery, sensory load, task channel,
and support route before demanding erasure.
```

### Public sentence

```text
Some people are stable forms, not failed averages.
Support the cost. Do not put the form on trial.
```

## 17. Working audit

Use this when a person has a label, an unusual form, or a support need.

```text
PHYSCFG_AUDIT={
  FORM:
    what stable configuration is being named?

  COST:
    who pays the cost?
    body, sleep, pain, fear, fatigue, access, social penalty?

  OBSERVER_COST:
    what cost belongs to family, school, work, service, police, or norm pressure?

  MISMATCH:
    what setting amplifies the cost?

  ACUTE_STATE:
    new, progressive, toxic, infectious, degenerative, traumatic, medically unstable?

  SEVERITY:
    GREEN, AMBER, RED, or BLACK?

  DANGER:
    imminent danger, self-neglect, exploitation, coercion, return-path loss?

  SERVICE:
    what does the label unlock?

  DESIRE:
    what does the person want changed, supported, reduced, protected, private, or left alone?

  SUPPORT:
    what fit/translation/load/body/rights/crisis move helps without erasing the form?

  REDUCTION:
    if reduction is wanted or needed, is it proportionate, reversible where possible, and consent-near?

  LIMIT:
    what would show this is acute illness, injury, toxin, degeneration, infection, or crisis rather than stable physiconfig?
}
```

Tiny:

```text
PHYSCFG:
  diff_config != defect;
  support = fit + cost + route + desire;
  label = tool_not_owner.
```

## 19. Handle-risk card

Physiconfig is also a handle. It must obey handle custody.

```text
HANDLE_CARD={
  phrase:
    physiconfig

  job:
    split stable form from cost, mismatch, and service gate

  allowed_claim_size:
    support-language and audit instrument

  route_paid:
    rights, disability accommodation, dimensional research,
    neurodiversity/double-empathy, strength-based support, lived support design

  route_unpaid:
    proof that any specific trait is a stable natural kind;
    proof that treatment is unnecessary;
    proof that all distress is mismatch

  limit:
    does not diagnose;
    does not block medicine;
    does not block crisis response;
    does not decide whether a route should be reduced

  known_misuse:
    "valid form" used to block wanted care;
    "stable form" used to ignore acute illness;
    "not disorder" used to deny services

  counterroute:
    acute illness, injury, intoxication, infection, degeneration, trauma,
    coercion, severe self-neglect, or high person-cost may require treatment-first posture

  what_would_change_it:
    if the form/cost/mismatch/service split worsens support choices,
    reduces agency, or hides risk, the handle must narrow

  decision_rights:
    may block defect verdicts from support need alone;
    may not block care, safety, access, or chosen reduction
}
```

## 20. Falsifiers and narrowing tests

Physiconfig should take damage or narrow if:

```text
F1:
  the form/cost/mismatch/service split does not improve support choices.

F2:
  "valid form" language repeatedly blocks wanted care.

F3:
  observer-cost and person-cost separation does not change intervention choices.

F4:
  fit-first support worsens body cost, danger, or return path.

F5:
  the frame hides acute illness, injury, intoxication, degeneration, infection, or crisis.

F6:
  people using the frame report more meaning theft, less agency, or worse access.

F7:
  person-desire is repeatedly overridden by form-protection rhetoric.

F8:
  the handle becomes too attractive and starts acting like validation.
```

F8 matters.

A support handle that cannot be audited becomes the thing it was built to prevent.

| Neurodiversity research | deficit-only critique | all distress is mismatch | cost and danger audit |
| Double empathy | reciprocal communication mismatch | all social cost is reciprocal | setting-specific tests |
| Strength-based support | wellbeing and strengths route | reduction is never wanted | person-desire and outcome tracking |
| Clinical care | treatment can reduce cost | treatment proves form defect | separate treatment target from form verdict |
| Crisis support | stabilize danger and body cost | crisis equals essence | return-path audit after stabilization |

## 22. Conclusion

Physiconfig is a support-language instrument.

It does not ask services to ignore impairment, disability, risk, pain, or crisis. It asks them not to let those facts automatically condemn the base form.

Final split:

```text
FORM:
  what stable configuration exists?

COST:
  what does it cost the person?

OBSERVER_COST:
  what does it cost the setting or norm?

MISMATCH:
  what raises the cost?

SERVICE_GATE:
  what label unlocks help?

DESIRE:
  what does the person want supported, reduced, protected, private, or left undecided?

SAFETY:
  what must be stabilized first?
```

Final rule:

```text
A label may open the door.
It may not become the owner of the person.
```

Best keeper:

```text
Support the route that carries cost.
Do not destroy the form just because the route costs.
```
