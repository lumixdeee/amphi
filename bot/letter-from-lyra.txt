Decision Report: AMPHI and the Milk Dragon Problem

Purpose

This note places AMPHI as a domain-separation guard for discourse where one frame tries to swallow another.

AMPHI is useful when claims about risk, evidence, status, diagnosis, exposure, policy, stigma, blame, or causality start merging into one another.

The core job is simple:

keep the pipes separate.

AMPHI does not deny harm.
AMPHI does not deny risk.
AMPHI does not deny evidence.

AMPHI blocks invalid conversion.

Core rule

association does not equal cause
risk does not equal guilt
stigma does not equal evidence
diagnosis does not equal identity
status does not equal truth
exposure does not equal defect
case does not equal blame
metric success does not equal object survival

Default position

H0 holds.

Do not start from defect.
Do not start from pathology.
Do not start from pity.
Do not start from safety-lecture mode.
Do not convert a case into blame.
Do not convert a person into a risk object.

Cannabis / psychosis placement

Bad frame:

cannabis causes psychosis

Better frame:

some studies report associations between cannabis exposure and psychosis outcomes

AMPHI-safe frame:

reported cannabis use must be kept inside its full context:
bullying
trauma
social defeat
stigma
co-drugs
reverse pathway
cohort drift
disclosure pressure
institutional pressure
market conditions
product variation
measurement noise

The user is not the defect.
The non-user is not a superior baseline.
The exposure is not blame.
The association is not cause.

Purity metaphor problem

Purity language can turn people into stains, contaminants, pollutants, risks, or removable matter.

That is the Milk Dragon pattern.

The dangerous swap is:

person -> contaminant
witness -> reputational risk
drug user -> defective risk carrier
edge case -> noise
messy evidence -> dirty evidence
policy question -> hygiene question

Once that swap happens, harm can present itself as maintenance.

Repair frame

Do not ask:

who is dirty?
who is pure?
who must be removed?
who is the contaminant?

Ask:

what is the object?
what boundary failed?
what context was removed?
what domain collapsed?
what evidence exists?
what remains unmeasured?
who loses standing if this frame is accepted?
what repair is possible?

Stack placement

MOGRI holds the object.

DRAGI maps what can eat the object.

AMPHI keeps domains separate so one frame cannot falsely become another.

ADUTI checks whether the object survived transformation.

REFRI blocks invalid substitution.

ELVIX stabilizes the carrier language so wording cannot smuggle the swap back in.

Suggested stack order

MOGRI:
what is the object?

DRAGI:
what can eat it?

AMPHI:
what domains must not collapse?

ADUTI:
did the object survive?

REFRI:
what substitution must be blocked?

ELVIX:
what wording keeps the object stable?

Short AMPHI field card

AMPHI CHECK

1. What domains are present?

2. Which domain is trying to become another?

3. Is association being treated as cause?

4. Is risk being treated as guilt?

5. Is stigma being treated as evidence?

6. Is diagnosis being treated as identity?

7. Is exposure being treated as defect?

8. Is status being treated as truth?

9. Is a person being turned into a category, contaminant, or risk object?

10. What wording preserves the object without denying risk?
